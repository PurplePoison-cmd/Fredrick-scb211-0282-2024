Grilled Chicken

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  2. Brush chicken breasts with olive oil and season with garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Grill each side for 6-7 minutes, or until fully cooked.
  4. Serve with your favorite side dish and enjoy!
